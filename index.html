Nelson Mandela

The hero of Africa.

Mandela visits Hlengiwe School ,South Africa.

Here is a brief timeline of Mandela's life:

  • 1918 - Born in Mvezo village,in the Eastern Cape.

  • 1925 - Attends primary school near Qunu(receives the name ‘Nelson’ from a teacher).

  • 1930 - Father dies.Entrusted to Thembu Regent Jongintaba Dalindyebo at the age of 12.

  • 1939 - Joins the University College of Fort Hare ,in Alice and is expelled the following year.

  • 1942 - Completes his BA at the University of South Africa and starts attending ANC meetings informally.

  • 1944 - Co-founds the ANC Youth League and marries Evelyn Ntoko Mase.

  • 1952 - Arrested and charged for violating the suppression of Communism act

  • 1958 - Divorces Evelyn Mase and marries Nomzamo Winnie Madikizela

  • 1960 - State of emergency is imposed and he is among thousands detained

  • 1962 - leaves the country for military training and to garner support for ANC.Returns and is arrested and sentenced to 5 years in prison for incitement and leaving the country without a passport.

  • 1963 - Sent to Robben Island and then appears in court for the first time in what is called the Rivonia Trial

  • 1964 - Sentenced to life and sent back to Robben island.

  • 1985 - Moved to Pollsmoor Prison.

  • 1990 - Released,ANC is unbanned and he is elected ANC deputy president.

  • 1993 - Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize.

  • 1994 - Votes for the first time in his life and is elected by parliament as the first president of a democratic South Africa.Launches his autobiography 'Long Walk to Freedom'.

  • 1995 - Establishes the Nelson Mandela Children's Fund

  • 1996 - Divorces Winnie Mandela.

  • 1999 - Steps down after one term as president.

  • 2003 - Establishes the Mandela Rhodes Foundation.

  • 2004 - Announces that he will be stepping down from public life.

  • 2013 - Passes away aged 95 at his home in Johannesburg.
